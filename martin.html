Martin Miranda

Martin grew up on the southside of Chicago. Martin’s family is originally from state of Guerrero in Mexico. He comes from a long line of farmers.Unfortunately he has 8 siblings. He has 4 sisters and 3 brothers. He attended Burroughs Elementary school in the Brighton Park neighborhood of Chicago and then transitioned on to Phoenix Military Academy High School on the northside of Chicago. After one year at Lake Forest College, he enrolled into the Year Up IT program to pursue a career in the IT field. He is currently a Year Up student and works part time at the National Opinion Research Center at the University of Chicago. He has a strong passion for learning History, philosophy, and IT.

Some of his hobbies are playing video games, playing the guitar, Billiards Pool, watching movies, and drinking coffee. His favorite video games are Bioshock Infinite and Resident Evil 4. He’s pretty okay with the guitar and at pool. His favorite movies are The Godfather and Goodfellas. He loves Colombian and Arabica coffee. He is trying to switch from coffee to tea.
